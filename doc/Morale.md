1. Base unmodified morale is 50%

### Morale checks

You don't always check morale, but when you do, check at the end
of the round.

If one side is hitting the other twice as often, or other displays
of similar superiority, check morale at the end of every round.

- Check when 25% of allies eliminated or slain; +5%
  - Or when individual takes 25% wound damage
- Leader unconscious: +10%
- 50% eliminated or slain: +15%
  - Or when individual takes 50% wound damage
- Leader slain or deserts: +30%
